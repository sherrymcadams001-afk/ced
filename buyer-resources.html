Quality Grading Standards

Softwood Grading (WWPA)

  • Select Structural: Highest strength, minimal defects
  • No. 1: High quality, limited knots
  • No. 2: Standard structural grade
  • No. 3: Economy grade, more defects
  • Stud: Vertical load-bearing only

Hardwood Grading (NHLA)

  • FAS: First and Seconds (83%+ clear)
  • FAS 1-Face: One face FAS, one face No.1
  • Select: One FAS face, 4" minimum
  • No. 1 Common: 66% clear, cabinet grade
  • No. 2A Common: 50% clear, paint grade
