Import/Export Logistics

Moving timber across borders requires expertise in phytosanitary regulations, customs documentation, and shipping logistics. Our international team handles the complexity so you can focus on your core business.

  • Customs Brokerage

    Pre-cleared documentation for smooth border crossings.

  • Phytosanitary Certificates

    ISPM-15 compliant heat treatment and fumigation.

  • Shipping Coordination

    Ocean freight, rail, and trucking logistics worldwide.

  • Multi-Currency Pricing

    Quotes in USD, CAD, EUR, AUD, and JPY.
