The Harvesting Workflow

Our precision forestry process ensures maximum timber value while protecting forest health

1

Timber Cruise

LiDAR-enabled forest inventory with species identification, volume estimation, and harvest planning.

LiDAR + GIS
2

Selective Felling

GPS-guided felling ensures precision cuts, minimizes damage to residual stands, and optimizes log value.

GPS Guided
3

Skidding & Yarding

Low-impact extraction using designated skid trails with slash mats to protect soil integrity.

Low Impact
4

Processing & Scaling

On-site processing, bucking, and certified scaling for accurate volume measurement and value optimization.

5

Reforestation

Site preparation and replanting with native species. 3-for-1 seedling program ensures forest regeneration.

3-for-1 Replant
