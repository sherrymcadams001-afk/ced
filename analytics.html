Dashboard Features

Real-Time Monitoring

Satellite imagery updates every 10 days to track canopy changes, disturbances, and growth patterns across your entire property.

Growth Projections

Proprietary models predict volume growth 5, 10, and 20 years into the future based on species, site index, and climate factors.

Market Analytics

Track regional timber prices, identify optimal harvest timing, and compare your returns to market benchmarks.

Health Indicators

Early detection of pest infestations, disease outbreaks, and drought stress through spectral analysis and machine learning.

ROI Tracking

Monitor your investment performance over time, including timber appreciation, carbon credit income, and management costs.

Mobile App

Access your forest data on the go with our iOS and Android apps. Field mapping, photo documentation, and offline access.
